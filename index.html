Getting Here

Travel to Humla

Humla is a remote and rewarding destination in western Nepal. Our team will guide you step-by-step and assist with local arrangements once you arrive.

✈
By Flight
Fly from Kathmandu (KTM) or Nepalgunj (KEP) to Simikot Airport (IMK) — the main gateway to Humla. Flights are seasonal; book in advance.
🚐
By Road
Road access via Surkhet or Nepalgunj with onward jeep/bus routes. This is a longer but scenic option for adventurous travelers.
🗺
Local Pickup & Guidance
Contact us before your journey — we'll help arrange your transfers from Simikot and guide you through all logistics.
